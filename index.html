About Our Brand

The Face of HerHair

Our journey began when our founder, Charlee Watkins, experienced her own hair transformation journey and realized the profound impact that premium quality hair units could have on a woman's self-esteem and personal expression.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

What types of hair products do you offer?

add

At HerHair, we specialize in high-quality hair extensions, wigs, and accessories. Our products are designed to enhance your natural beauty while providing versatility and style. We offer a wide range of textures, lengths, and colors to suit every preference.

How do I choose the right hair for me?

add

Choosing the right hair depends on your personal style, hair type, and desired look. We recommend considering factors such as texture, length, and color. Our customer service team is available to help you find the perfect match through personalized consultations.

Are your products ethically sourced?

add

Yes! At HerHair, we are committed to ethical sourcing practices. We ensure that all our hair products are obtained responsibly and with respect for the communities involved. We maintain strict standards throughout our supply chain.

How do I care for my hair extensions or wig?

add

We recommend using sulfate-free, gentle shampoos and conditioners specifically formulated for human hair extensions or wigs. Wash in lukewarm water, avoid excessive heat styling, and store properly when not in use. Each product comes with detailed care instructions tailored to that specific hair type.

What makes HerHair different from other hair brands?

add

HerHair stands out for its commitment to quality, affordability, and authenticity. Our products are designed with the modern, confident woman in mind, celebrating the beauty and strength of diverse hair types. We provide personalized consultations, custom styling options, and exceptional customer service throughout your hair journey.
